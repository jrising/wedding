9am - 12pm: Beach and rail trail bike ride (round-trip from 50 Long Pond Rd.)
Alyson will lead a roughly 25-30 mile recreational ride Saturday morning to Cape Cod's famed rail trail with a few stops for photo opps along the way. Suggested packing: water, snacks, sunscreen, shades, helmet, lock (in case we take a short walk on one of the beaches) and small backpack (if needed to carry supplies). This ride will involve some climbing over the outer bluffs of the cape via Long Pond Road to reach some beautiful ocean views, followed by some riding on the Rail Trail to some optional beach view stops. Ridewithgps cue sheet and elevation profile and Google Maps to bike rentals (Idle Times Bikes, opens at 9am).

12:30pm: Memorial Day Hackathon (Ron and Linda Goetzel’s House, 180 Eastwind Circle)
All are welcome to participate in the hackathon, whether you want to contribute writing, reading, coding, or art. The goal is to start building a national picture of the issues that can form the basis for local political campaigns: who cares about what, and where is there enough overlap to elect someone on those issues? We'll look at google search trends, local industries, environmental problems, and more.
