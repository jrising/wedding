Ferry and Shuttle Schedules

There are two ferry companies that offer 90 minute service between Boston and Provincetown: Boston Harbor Cruises and Bay State Cruises. Our shuttles are timed to align with the Bay State Cruise times, and taxis are available otherwise. Below is a comprehensive listing of the fast ferries that let you stay for the wedding, along with the shuttles that we are organizing.
