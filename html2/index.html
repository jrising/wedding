Facts and uncertainties

We selected to have our wedding in Wellfleet, a place of nostalgic and historical significance. Marconi Station, within Wellfleet, is the site of the first trans-Atlantic communication, between American and Europe in 1901.

This is very meaningful to us, both as a symbol, and key to our relationship: communication and international travel. More on the history can be found at the numerous bookstores along the Cape, where James will be is sitting, deep in thought.

Johanna and her extended family have been coming here for years. The beach in her happy place: swimming, sunshine, farm stand fruit and time with family.

We are delighted to share this special location with you, as a site to build new memories.
